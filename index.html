Open Human
Factors Science

A student-led initiative out of the University of Toronto, we aim to promote Open Science principles and practice within the field of human factors and ergonomics.

Reading Group

Currently open to registered students at the University of Toronto, this group meets monthly to discuss current literature in human factors and ergonomics. Discussions will prompt students to engage with how authors have incorporated Open Science in their work or how incorporating Open Science could strengthen their work.

Adopting Open Science in Human Factors and Ergonomics

Drawing on interviews with an interdisciplinary set of researchers, practitioners, and students in human factors and ergonomics and a scan of literature, initiatives, and infrastructures, this working paper reviews the state of Open Science in the field and calls on the community to adopt Open Science.
